Our Story

A Heritage
of Flavour

Grand China is where ancient culinary tradition meets contemporary elegance. From the moment you step beneath our crimson lanterns and past the stone guardian lions at the entrance, you are transported to a world shaped by centuries of Chinese gastronomy.

Our kitchen honours time-tested recipes with the freshest ingredients — from slow-braised aromatic duck and hand-formed dumplings to silky wonton broths and fragrant noodle dishes inspired by every corner of China.

Whether you visit for an intimate dinner, a family celebration, or a set banquet with friends, every detail at Grand China is crafted to create a truly memorable experience.
